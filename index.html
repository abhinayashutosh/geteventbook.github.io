Event Book
Calendars made easy
Event Book is a free, Apple-featured, calendar app that combines traditional calendar events with maps, weather, and location to keep you on top of your schedule
Connect with Event Book
Sync instantly with all your existing iOS calendars, including Google, iCloud, Yahoo, Exchange, Hotmail, AOL, and more!
No set-up required.
Event Book’s unique Day, Week, Month, List, and Location views allow you to check out and plan for your events like never before, all in the view of your choice.
A beautiful, and ever-growing, collection of themes allows you to see your calendars like never before. Choose a theme to match your mood, the weather, or the time of day.
Maps and weather integration get you where you need to go, with directions from your choice of Apple or Google Maps, all while staying one step ahead of the weather.
